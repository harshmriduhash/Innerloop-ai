# InnerLoop.ai GTM & Launch Strategy

## 🚀 The Launch Strategy
We will follow a "Build-in-Public" approach to create early hype and trust.

### Phase 1: The First 20 Beta Customers (Days 1–15)
- **Goal:** Get initial feedback and social proof.
- **Method:** Warm outreach to "Power Users" on Twitter (Build-in-Public community), IndieHackers, and Reddit (`r/productivity`, `r/Journaling`).
- **Offer:** "Founding Member" lifetime discount (e.g., $49 for life) or 6 months free.

### Phase 2: Reaching 100+ Users (Days 16–45)
- **Goal:** Validate the product-market fit.
- **Method:** 
    - **SEO Content:** Write 3 blog posts on "The Science of Reflection" and "Voice Journaling vs Typing."
    - **Directory Listings:** Submit to `Toolfind.ai`, `Ben's Bites`, `There's An AI For That`.
    - **Micro-Influencers:** Send personalized Loom videos to productivity YouTubers/Twitter personalities.

## 🎨 Branding & Marketing Advice
**Should you spend on Ads?**
- **NO initially.** Ads work best when you have a high converting funnel. 
- **Focus on Organic first.** Since this is a "Self-Care / Productivity" tool, trust and community are more important than cold impressions.
- **Strategy:** Become the "Voice Memory" person on social media. Share your own InnerLoop metrics.

### Key Branding Pillars
1. **Privacy First:** "We don't sell your thoughts."
2. **Minimalism:** "Less features, more reflection."
3. **Elegant High-End:** The "Midnight Gold" aesthetic must feel like a premium ritual.
