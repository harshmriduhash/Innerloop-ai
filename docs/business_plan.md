# InnerLoop.ai Business Plan & Financial Projections

## Product Viability Assessment
**Is this a real product?** YES.
**Why?**
- **The "Memory Gap":** General LLMs (ChatGPT) are "stateless" by default for most users. InnerLoop solves the "what did I say last week?" problem.
- **Voice-First Trend:** Users are increasingly using voice notes. Transforming these into structured "memories" is a high-value utility.
- **Niche Focus:** Unlike broad assistants, InnerLoop focuses on *Personal Reflection* and *Decision History*.

## 📈 Scalability Potential
- **B2B Potential:** Selling to "Life Coaches" or "Therapists" as a tool for their clients to track reflections between sessions.
- **High Retention:** As the "Memory Bank" grows, the switching cost for the user increases (Data Moat).
